What is Dropbox for Zoho CRM?

Dropbox for Zoho CRM is a Zoho Marketplace extension that integrates Dropbox directly inside Zoho CRM. It acts as a Dropbox CRM connector, allowing you to sync Dropbox with Zoho, automate Dropbox folders, preview files, and manage document workflows without leaving your CRM dashboard.

Why Integrate Dropbox with Zoho CRM?

1. Reduce Context Switching

Employees spend nearly 20% of their time searching for documents. Integrating Dropbox with Zoho CRM centralizes file access.

2. Automate Folder Creation

Save 5–10 minutes per record with automated Dropbox folder generation.

3. Improve Collaboration

Generate share links directly from CRM for faster client communication.

4. Structured Cloud Storage

Maintain organized nested folder structures linked to CRM records.

5. Enterprise-Ready Security

Works seamlessly with Dropbox Business and enterprise-grade compliance.
