FAQ

 

Why do I need Zoho CRM extensions if I already use Zoho CRM?

Zoho CRM covers core CRM features, but extensions let you automate extra workflows, connect with other tools (telephony, SMS, storage, etc.), and solve specific business problems without custom development. They save time, reduce manual work, and improve adoption.

 

Which Zoho CRM extension should I install first?

It depends on your biggest pain point:

  • Need better communication? → SMS Magic or other WhatsApp/SMS tools

  • Document chaos? → WorkDrive for Zoho CRM

  • Attachment safety? → Easy Attachment Backup for Zoho CRM

  • Better emailing? → Easy Email for Zoho CRM

  • Segmentation & targeting? → List Manager for Zoho CRM

  • Accurate addresses? → Google Address Toolkit for Zoho CRM

Start with the extension that removes the most manual work for your team.

 

Are these Zoho CRM extensions difficult to set up?

Most marketplace extensions (including SMS Magic, WorkDrive, List Manager, Google Address Toolkit, Easy Email, SharePoint, and Easy Attachment Backup) are designed to be plug-and-play:

  1. Install from Zoho Marketplace

  2. Grant permission

  3. Configure a few settings

  4. Start using in CRM

No heavy coding or complex deployment is required for most use cases.

 

Will these extensions slow down my Zoho CRM?

No, if used correctly. Most extensions run on demand (when you open a widget, click a button, or trigger an action) or in the background via APIs. In many cases (WorkDrive, Easy Attachment Backup), they actually improve performance by reducing CRM file storage.

 

Are these extensions safe for my data?

Extensions like WorkDrive for Zoho CRM, SharePoint for Zoho CRM, and Easy Attachment Backup for Zoho CRM store files in your own cloud accounts (Zoho WorkDrive, Microsoft 365/SharePoint).
Security still depends on:

  • Your Zoho CRM permissions

  • Your cloud platform’s security settings

  • How you configure access roles and sharing

Always review the extension’s permissions and documentation before going live.

 

What types of businesses benefit most from WorkDrive for Zoho CRM?

Any business with lots of documents attached to CRM records, especially:

  • Real estate & property

  • Construction & projects

  • Legal & consulting

  • Agencies & creative services

  • Healthcare & clinics

  • Manufacturing & engineering

They use WorkDrive for Zoho CRM to auto-create folders, organize files, and keep documents accessible by record.

 

What’s the difference between Easy Attachment Backup and WorkDrive for Zoho CRM?

  • WorkDrive for Zoho CRM → Focuses on document management & daily file usage (viewing, organizing, working with files).

  • Easy Attachment Backup for Zoho CRM → Focuses on data protection & backup (ensuring attachments are safely copied to cloud and not lost).

Many teams use both:
WorkDrive for everyday file management + Easy Attachment Backup for safety and historical backup.

 

How does List Manager for Zoho CRM help my marketing and sales teams?

List Manager for Zoho CRM lets you create, manage, and segment dynamic lists of leads/contacts based on criteria such as:

  • Industry

  • Region

  • Stage

  • Activity

  • Custom fields

This is extremely useful for targeted campaigns, cold outreach, and follow-ups, instead of sending the same message to everyone.

 

Is SMS Magic better than built-in email for follow-ups?

Not better — but different and often faster for certain use cases.
Email is still key, but SMS Magic gives you:

  • Faster response rates

  • Short, direct messages

  • WhatsApp/SMS templates

  • Real-time two-way conversations

Many teams use both: Easy Email for rich email communication + SMS Magic for quick reminders & follow-ups.

 

How do I choose the best Zoho CRM extensions for my business?

Ask these questions:

  1. Where are we wasting the most time? (documents, emailing, manual lists, messaging, etc.)

  2. Which process has the most errors or confusion?

  3. Which area, if automated, will give us the biggest ROI?

Then match:

  • Documents → WorkDrive for Zoho CRM / SharePoint for Zoho CRM

  • Attachments & backup → Easy Attachment Backup for Zoho CRM

  • Email productivity → Easy Email for Zoho CRM

  • Messaging → SMS Magic

  • Segmentation → List Manager for Zoho CRM

  • Address accuracy → Google Address Toolkit
