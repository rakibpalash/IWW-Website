Easy Dropbox for Zoho CRM is a powerful integration that automatically syncs attachments from Zoho CRM to Dropbox in real time.

You get:

✔ Automatic attachment uploads
✔ Organized record-wise folders
✔ Historical attachment syncing
✔ Cloud-based accessibility
✔ Optional auto-delete from CRM to free storage

It’s the simplest way to modernize your file system.
