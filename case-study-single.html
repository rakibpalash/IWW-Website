This case-study style page shows how a consulting-led Zoho CRM rollout can improve sales visibility, team handoffs, and reporting confidence without forcing teams into generic processes.

At a Glance

A delivery model built around workflow clarity, not just CRM setup.

The engagement focused on aligning pipeline stages, reducing duplicate work, and giving leadership a more reliable view of sales activity and progress across the business.

Challenge

Generic configuration was not enough for the way the team actually operated.

Pipeline stages, reporting needs, and handoff processes required deeper alignment than a standard implementation could offer.

Key friction points

  • Sales workflows that did not map cleanly to CRM stages
  • Reporting that lacked confidence and consistency
  • Process handoffs that depended too heavily on manual follow-up
